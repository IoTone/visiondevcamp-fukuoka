VisionDevCamp Fukuoka

A historic hackathon from Silicon Valley since 2007 is coming to Fukuoka for the first time!

VisionDevCamp is a non-profit developer event focused on application development for Apple Vision Pro and visionOS. An experienced volunteer team supports developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and enthusiasts of Apple Vision Pro and visionOS.

Highlights (Free Event)

  • Prizes: 1 x ¥10000 Amazon Gift Card, 2 x ¥5000 JCB gift cards, 1 x Solar Charger mobile devices 4 x ¥2000 gift cards
  • Hardware Hackathon Prizes: 1 x M5Stack Camera, 1 x M5StampS3, 1 x Arduino
  • Lunch provided (meetup style pizza Sat / Sun), Hacker friendly Snacks + Energy Drinks
  • VisionPro Expert Mentors in Design, Swift, Unity from Silicon Valley, Australia, and Tokyo

Also held in Tokyo!

Event Schedule

Day 1 - April 11

17:00-19:00 REGISTRATION

  • Signin/Registration/Spatial Mingle

19:00-19:30 MIXER

  • Mixer + Food

19:30-20:30 Keynote

  • Tokyo/Fukuoka simulcast

20:30-22:30 Pitch Round/Intro/Team Formation

  • This is a chance to speak to the group and pitch an idea, ask for help, look for a team. We will spend most of our time on this ! Please attend in person.

22:30 Fukuoka Evening Closing Remarks/Next Place

  • We will offer closing remarks, and give everyone an opportunity to gather to go grab a quick bite outside in Tenjin and possibly wander off to “the next place”. Depending on access, it may be possible to work late on site.

Day 2 - April 12

9:00 Breakfast

  • Japanese Breakfast. Coffee Available at the Cafe or nearby Convenience Store.

9:30 Opening Remarks

  • Opening Remarks.

Hacking @ 10:00 -> Lunch (13:00) -> Dinner (19:00)

  • Open Hacking Session. Lunch at 1PM. Dinner at 17:00. Some people will lead groups to go out for food or drink during the dinner hour if you prefer outside food.

Fukuoka Saturday Evening Closing Remarks/Next Place 22:30

  • We will offer closing remarks, and give everyone an opportunity to gather to go grab a quick bite outside in Tenjin and possibly wander off to “the next place”. Depending on access, it may be possible to work late on site.

Day 3 - April 13

Hacking Wrap Up

  • Hacking Session and wrap up your projects and submit by 14:00!!!

Judging/Hackathon Demos

  • Judges will convene. All participant teams will demo their submissions. Please use a real AVP for the demos.

Winners Circle/Awards Ceremony

  • 16:00 After judging, Awards will be announced. Tune in online or in person.

zzz Celebrate Dinner/Post Party

  • After awards, teams are free to go home or head out to dinner together. A post party will be sponsored at 20:30 in Tenjin. Participants will be offered free drinks until 22:00. Location TBA.
